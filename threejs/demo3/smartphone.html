About This Demo

This interactive 3D Smartphone demo showcases the capabilities of Three.js for creating and manipulating realistic 3D models. The demo features a detailed smartphone model with customizable properties, materials, and environment settings.

Key features of this smartphone model viewer include:

  • Realistic 3D smartphone model with detailed components
  • Customizable colors, materials, and reflections
  • Interactive "explode view" to see internal components
  • Multiple environment and lighting options
  • Camera controls for viewing from different angles
  • Screenshot capability to save your custom designs

This demo demonstrates advanced Three.js techniques including:

  • Procedural 3D model generation
  • PBR (Physically Based Rendering) materials
  • Dynamic lighting and shadows
  • Post-processing effects
  • Interactive animations and transitions

Use the controls panel to customize the smartphone and explore different visual styles and configurations. You can also interact directly with the 3D model by clicking and dragging to rotate, scrolling to zoom, and right-clicking to pan.
